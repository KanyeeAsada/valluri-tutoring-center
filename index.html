About The Program

Valluri Tutoring Center provides structured online math bootcamps designed to help students strengthen foundational concepts, improve confidence, and prepare for future academic success.

About the Teacher

Kaushiki Valluri is a Computer Engineer who graduated with a Bachelor of Science from Virginia Tech. She pursued her Master's degree in Engineering Management from Cornell University.

She graduated from LCPS schools where she was heavily involved in academics, leadership, athletics, and extracurricular activities. Throughout her school years, she served as Class President, Homecoming Queen, Prom Queen, and Tennis Captain.

Kaushiki has been tutoring mathematics for over 10 years, including 7 years of online instruction. She has helped students build confidence, strengthen foundational math skills, and prepare for academic success.

She has received many testimonials and positive reviews from students and families she has worked with in the past. Today, she works for a renowned engineering company while continuing to expand Valluri Tutoring Center.

How Classes Work

All sessions are conducted live online through Zoom. Students can join from anywhere in the world. Structured lessons, guided practice, and homework support are included throughout the program.
