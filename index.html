About The Program

Valluri Tutoring Center provides structured online math bootcamps designed to help students strengthen foundational concepts, improve confidence, and prepare for future academic success.

Topics Covered

Algebra

Equations, graphing, functions, and problem solving.

Geometry

Angles, triangles, proofs, circles, and applications.

SAT Math

Strategies, timed practice, and score improvement.

Calculus Prep

Limits, derivatives, and foundational calculus concepts.

How Classes Work

All sessions are conducted live online through Zoom. Students can join from anywhere in the world. Structured lessons, guided practice, and homework support are included throughout the program.
